  12. The National Highway Safety Administration gave the Tesla Model S a 5.4/5 safety rating, the highest ever given to a car.
  13. Musk is one of the driving drivers behind SolarCity, which he and his cousins created. He is also the company's main shareholder.
  14. Elon Musk also created SpaceX (formerly known as Space Exploration Technologies), a corporation that designs and manufactures space launch vehicles, with a concentration on rocket technology. His goal is to lower the cost of space travel in order to expand human life beyond the Earth.
